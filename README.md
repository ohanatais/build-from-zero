# build-from-zero

> The complete guide to building a digital product from scratch — for people who want to understand every step, not just ship fast.

Most "build with AI" guides start at the code. This one starts before it, and goes all the way to launch.

`build-from-zero` is an open-source, end-to-end guide for autodidacts and aspiring founders who want to learn the full journey: **product thinking → design → marketing → building → go-to-market** — with the depth that actually makes the difference.

---

## Why this exists

The internet has no shortage of tutorials on *how to vibe-code an app in a weekend.*

What's missing is the layer underneath: the thinking that determines whether what you build is worth building at all.

This guide covers what gets skipped most often:

- Is this idea worth building? Does a market exist — or is it already saturated?
- Who is this for, exactly? What do they feel, fear, and want?
- How does the product stand for something? What's the brand, the voice, the copy?
- What does the product do, specifically? What's the PRD that guides the build?
- How do you actually build it without being a developer?
- How do you launch it to real people — and get them to stay?

Skip these, and you'll ship fast to nobody.

## Who this is for

- **Aspiring founders** who have ideas but don't know where to start
- **Non-technical builders** who want to understand every decision being made, not just follow steps
- **PMs** applying product thinking to their own side projects for the first time
- **Anyone** who's tried to build something and realized something critical was missing before the code

---

## How to use this

Follow the modules in order if you're starting from zero.

Jump to the stage you're at if you're already mid-build:
- Have an idea but not sure if it's worth it? → `01-product-thinking`
- Struggling with positioning, naming, or copy? → `02-brand-and-copy`
- Ready to build but lost in the technical decisions? → `03-building`
- Have something working and want to launch? → `04-go-to-market`
